NEW SESSION REGISTRATION IS OPEN!

On Site Archery JOAD Club is a registered member of USA Archery, serving ages 8 to 20. All skill levels are welcome! We offer 2 youth groups: beginner and intermediate/tournament. All youth archers must start in the beginner group unless approved by On Site Archery staff. Our adult archery program (AAP) is a registered member of USA Archery. Our adult program welcomes adults of all ages and skill levels.

Club activities range from beginner instruction and recreational shooting to training for competition. As archers progress, they will learn more advanced shooting techniques. Both JOAD and AAP have achievement award programs, where pins can be earned in our monthly pin shoots. Each pin level achieved represents an archer's progressing skills.

We encourage all archers to participate in club activities, pin shoots and tournaments when they are ready. To participate in tournaments, archers will need to own equipment.
